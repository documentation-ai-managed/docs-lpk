---
title: Cannot Access Dashboard
description: Solve login or dashboard loading issues in Lpk.
---

## Symptoms

- Dashboard will not load
- Stuck on loading screen
- Login repeatedly fails

## Fix Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your internet connection">
    ````bash
    ping app.lpk.com
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Clear cookies and local storage, then retry.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try incognito mode">
    Sometimes extensions block authentication.
  </Step>
</Steps>
