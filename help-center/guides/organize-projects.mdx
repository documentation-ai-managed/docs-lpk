## Overview

Structure your Lpk documentation for clarity.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Your Project">
    Open your Lpk dashboard and select a project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Sections and Pages">
    Use the sidebar to create new sections like "Introduction", "API", or "Guides".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drag to Reorder">
    Drag sections or pages to rearrange the documentation structure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish Changes">
    Click "Publish" so your team can see updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>
